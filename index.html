About Us

We are Ambitious, Achievers, Players, Pride and Future of our nation. Our teams have experienced members as well as learning and growing youngsters who have a dream to play for the honour of their country. This academy started back in 2017. From coaching them on the ins and outs of the sport to honing their hockey skills, we are proud to teach not only the love of the game but also impart the ideals of good sportsmanship, teamwork and loyalty. Our shoulders have the responsibility of bringing Joy, Dignity and Satisfaction to the ones having a love for their national sport. We work together to help sincere players take part in competitive tournaments that we are proud of for folks that we believe in. Our ultimate motto is winning gold in Olympic with a team no less than 11 Dhyan chand.
